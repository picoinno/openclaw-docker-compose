# Marketing Skill

## Description
Marketing operations: campaign planning, content creation, social media management, analytics, and brand strategy. Extends the social-media skill with broader marketing capabilities.

## When to Use
- Planning marketing campaigns (content calendar, channels, budget)
- Drafting marketing content (posts, emails, landing page copy)
- Analyzing campaign performance and engagement metrics
- Competitor analysis and market positioning
- Brand voice and messaging consistency reviews

## Capabilities
- Draft content for multiple platforms (social, email, blog)
- Build content calendars with scheduling
- Analyze engagement data and conversion funnels
- Segment audiences and personalize messaging
- Track campaign ROI and attribution

## Integration
- Uses social-media skill for Facebook/LinkedIn/Twitter execution
- Uses Data agent for analytics and reporting
- Uses Docu agent for content documentation

## Rules
- All public content is DRAFT until human approves — never auto-publish
- Match tone to platform and audience
- Track metrics: reach, engagement, conversion, cost per acquisition
- A/B test messaging when possible
- Respect brand guidelines — maintain voice consistency
- Include call-to-action in every customer-facing piece
